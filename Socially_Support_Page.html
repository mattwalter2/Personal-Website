Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I start using Socially?
A: Download the app from the App Store, sign up for an account, and start learning through conversation!

Q: How can I find events in my area?
A: Navigate to the home screen and click on an event to view details and find events in your area.

Q: How do I create an event?
A: Click the plus icon (+) on the home screen to create a new event. Fill in the required details and save.

Q: Can I save events to view later?
A: Yes, you can save events within the app. To view your saved events, go to the "My Event" tab.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues such as difficulty logging in or audio problems, try restarting the app or reinstalling if the problem persists.

Updates

Keep your app up-to-date by enabling automatic updates in your device settings or visiting the App Store for the latest version.
