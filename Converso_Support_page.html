Converso Support

Welcome to the Converso Support page. If you have any questions or need assistance with the Converso app, we're here to help.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I start using Converso?
A: Download the app from the App Store, sign up for an account, and start learning through conversation!

Q: How can I access different languages?
A: On the home screen, click on the language that you would like to practice conversing in.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues such as difficulty logging in or audio problems, try restarting the app or reinstalling if the problem persists.

User Guides

For detailed guides on how to maximize your learning, visit our User Guides section on the website or check out our video tutorials.

Updates

Keep your app up-to-date by enabling automatic updates in your device settings or visiting the App Store for the latest version.
